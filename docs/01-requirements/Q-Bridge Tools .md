This tool is designed to:

1. Evaluate staff performance in teaching, research, community service, and professional conduct;

The process involves:

* Self-assessment by the staff member.  
* Supervisor (HoD) review and comments.  
* Joint discussion and submission to the Quality Assurance Unit (QAU).
